Biography

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University (2023-), working with Prof. Wenwu Zhu and Prof. Xin Wang at the Department of Computer Science and Technology. I am a visiting student at Simon Fraser University (2021.7-2022.7), working with Prof. Jiangchuan Liu at the School of Computer Science. I received my Ph.D. from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (2018-2023), supervised by Prof. Jianxin Liao, Prof. Jingyu Wang and Prof. Qi Qi at the State Key Laboratory of Networking and Switching Technology, School of Computer and Science. My recently research interests include environtment learning, autonomous systems, and multimedia transmission.
